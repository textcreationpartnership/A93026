#####Front#####
TWO SEASONABLE DISCOURSES Concerning this preſent Parliament.OXFORD, Printed in the Year, 1675.
#####Body#####

1. The Debate or Arguments for Diſſolving this preſent Parliament, and the calling frequent and new Parliaments.

1. A List of the Lords who were preſent at the Vote for an Addreſs to the King for Diſſolving the Parliament, November the 20th. 1675.

1. The Proteſtation, with Reaſons of ſeveral Lords for the Diſsolution of this Parliament, entred in the Lords Journal, November 22. 1675. the day the Parliament was Prorogued.
